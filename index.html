Modular, stackable connectivity for the future of electronics

What is ChainBus?

ChainBus is an open electric standard designed for stackable modules, enabling seamless power and data connectivity between electronic components. Whether you're building custom devices, prototyping, or scaling up production, ChainBus simplifies modular design.

Key Features

  • Easy-to-use stackable connectors
  • Reliable power and data transmission
  • Open standard for maximum compatibility
  • Ideal for rapid prototyping and scalable products
